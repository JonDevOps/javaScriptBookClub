Level up your JavaScript skills

We will build our confidence by learning JavaScript together and building our own projects! Here are the steps to fully join the book club:

  1. Join the Discord channel above and introduce yourself.
  2. Subscribe to our youtube channel above because that's where we will post our daily meetup videos for those who miss a day.
  3. Fill out the google form below so I can add you to the calendar
  4. Buy the current book somewhere.
  5. Read 25 pages a day from the current book on the list and discuss it in the Discord daily meeting.
  6. Join the club and start building projects, learning, and meeting like minded individuals!
